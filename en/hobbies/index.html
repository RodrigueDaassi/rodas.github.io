Hobbies


In my free time, I enjoy playing the piano, saxophone, and singing, with a particular passion for gospel music. I have also served as a choir master, leading and directing choirs in various performances. My love for music extends beyond just playing instruments; it is a way for me to connect with my community and express my creativity.

Additionally, I have a passion for reading, cycling, and engaging in meaningful conversations with people. I believe in the power of communication and sharing knowledge, which helps me build strong relationships and contribute positively to my community.

Beyond music and other activities, I have a keen interest in various other pursuits. My involvement in the Association des Béninois de Québec showcases my dedication to fostering cultural connections and supporting community initiatives. Additionally, I enjoy exploring new technologies and applying my analytical skills to personal projects, ranging from experimental design development to innovative applications in smart farming.

My commitment to sustainable practices is not limited to my professional work. I actively seek out ways to incorporate eco-friendly habits into my daily life, whether through gardening, recycling, or promoting environmental awareness among my peers.

For me, hobbies are not just pastimes; they are integral to my overall well-being and professional growth. They provide a balance that allows me to return to my research with renewed energy and fresh perspectives.
