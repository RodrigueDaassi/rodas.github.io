Biography

Welcome to my personal webpage!

I am a tenure track Researcher at Biopterre - Centre de bioproduits et d'innovation. Currently, I contribute my skills as a researcher at Biopterre, focusing on the in vitro multiplication of carnivorous plants, the valorization of cannabis residue extracts for the biological control of soil fungi, and the isolation and characterization of lignins from agricultural residues. My work also extends to the production of nanoparticles and innovative applications in the field of smart agriculture.

I received my PhD in Wood Engineering and Biosourced Materials from Université Laval in 2022.
